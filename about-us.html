About Us

Live Coffee Making Process

Located in the heart of Parramatta, The Aroma Mocha Café offers a unique dining experience that allows you to enjoy the vibrant atmosphere of the area.

The Aroma Mocha Café combines modern Australian architecture with a traditional bohemian feel that adds genuine warmth. From our second level dining area, to our cosy downstairs booths to the al fresco area.

The Aroma Mocha Café is the perfect location to soak up the hustle and bustle of Parramatta.

Backed by over 45 years' experience, our hand roasted fresh, gourmet coffee beans are skilfully prepared by our baristars to tantalise your taste buds.

So come and join us with a cup of our freshly blended coffee!
